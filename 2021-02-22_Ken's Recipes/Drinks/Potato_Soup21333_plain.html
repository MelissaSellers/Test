Potato Soup21333

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • Dash salt
  • Dash pepper
  • 2 tablespoons parsley flakes
  • 1 quart cubed potatoes
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions

  • Brown bacon and onions. Drain fat and add to potatoes.
  • Cover with just enough water to cook until potatoes are tender.
  • Add sour cream and chicken soup, and enough milk to achieve desired consistency. Heat until hot, but do not boil.
  • Sprinkle with parsley flakes.
  • Refrigerate overnight to achieve better flavor.
