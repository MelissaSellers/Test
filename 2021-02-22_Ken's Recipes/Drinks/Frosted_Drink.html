Frosted Drink

1984

    1/4 cup Tang or other mix
   2 tablespoons sugar
   1 cup water
   1/2 cup milk
   1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
   2 cups crushed ice


Mix ingredients and blend.

To crush ice, wrap in towel and hammer.

Can mix with fresh fruit.
