Chicken & Rice Casserole

1986

Flour mix: 1/2 cup flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt, dash of pepper.

Brown 1 cut-up fryer (which has been rolled in flour mix) in butter slightly.

Put in casserole with: 1 1/2 cup uncooked rice, 1 pkg onion soup mix, & 1 can cream of chicken soup; pour over this 3 cups of water.

Bake 2 hours at 325°. Check to add water if needed. Stir rice and flip chicken during baking.
