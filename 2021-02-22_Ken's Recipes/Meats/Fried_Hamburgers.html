Fried Hamburgers

1984

Mix 1 - 2 pounds ground meat, 1/3 - 1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

Make "snowball" & flatten into patty.

Heat on medium skillet; turn down if burning. Usually 4 patties per skillet.

Flip and cook other side.

Put cheese slices on patties just before removing from skillet.

Cut tomatoes, lettuce, & pickles before cooking.

Good with beans & chips.

Can add 1 cup Hunts BBQ sauce to meat.
