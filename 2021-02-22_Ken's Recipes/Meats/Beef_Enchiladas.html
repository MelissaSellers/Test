Beef Enchiladas


1986

    2 teaspoons worcestershire sauce
   1/2 teaspoon salt
   1 medium onion, chopped
   2 cups Velveeta cheese (or cheddar cheese)
   1 can Wolf Brand Chili
   1 package tortillas


Brown seasoned hamburger meat in medium hot skillet; cook onions til clear; get meat out of skillet, leaving grease.

Soften tortillas with grease in skillet, flipping about 1 second on each side.

Put some meat and grated cheese in each tortilla; secure with toothpick.

Arrange enchiladas in greased platter.

Heat in oven 30 - 45 minutes in 350° oven.

Good with refried beans, mexican rice, and salad.

Tortillas: heat in 300° oven in tinfoil.
