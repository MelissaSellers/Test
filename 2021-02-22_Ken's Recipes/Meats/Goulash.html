Goulash

1985

    2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
   1 teaspoon salt
   1 pound ground beef
   1 - 2 medium squash, diced
   2 cups okra, diced
   2 medium onions, diced
   1 28-ounce can tomatoes
   1 8-ounce can tomato sauce
   1 beef bouillon cube
   1/3 cup rice
   1/3 cup macaroni


In hot skillet, brown meat with worcestershire sauce and salt. Add onion, then okra, then squash, stirring constantly.

When the okra and onions are "soft," add tomatoes, sauce, and bouillon cube and simmer on low for 15 minutes.

May need to add water, 1 cup at a time.
