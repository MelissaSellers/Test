Homemade Soup

1985

    1 teaspoon salt
   1 beef bouillon cube
   1 package beef stew meat, cut in small pieces
   1 28-ounce can tomatoes
   1 can tomato sauce
   1 - 2 carrots, chopped
   1 - 2 onions, chopped
   5 medium potatoes, chopped
   1 stick celery, chopped
   1/3 cup rice
   1/3 cup macaroni
   1/3 cup green peas


Heat meat to boiling in large thick pot; cook on low 30 minutes.

Add other ingredients and cook til done (2 hours).

Add water occasionally as needed.
