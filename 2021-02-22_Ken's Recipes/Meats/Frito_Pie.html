Frito Pie

1983

Grease large shallow pan & Layer:

Fritos or Doritos; chili with or without beans (large can); Velveeta or cheddar cheese (thin strips or shredded); chopped onions.

Heat & Serve.

350° for 15 minutes. Good with lettuce & tomato salad.
