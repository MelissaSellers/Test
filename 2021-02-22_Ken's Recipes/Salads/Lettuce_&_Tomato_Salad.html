Lettuce & Tomato Salad

1983

Tear off outer lettuce leaves. Wash, drain, tear inner leaves and put in salad bowl.

Add chopped tomato (1 or 2), 1/4 cup onion, and a little bell pepper, celery, and/or "cukes" as desired.

Season with 1/3 cup vinegar and 1/3 cup mayonnaise.

Mix and chill til served.
