Taco Salad

1984

    1 cup ground beef
   1 cup pinto beans (or "Ranch-Style" beans), canned, drained
   1 cup lettuce
   1 cup diced tomatoes
   Shredded cheddar cheese
   Chopped onion, to taste
   1 cup Doritos or Fritos


Brown ground beef in skillet, drain, and cool.

Mix all ingredients lightly, and serve cold.

May also use French dressing.
