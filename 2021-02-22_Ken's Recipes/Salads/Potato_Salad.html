Potato Salad

1984

    3 large potatoes
   5 eggs
   1/2 teaspoon salt
   1 tablespoon vinegar (to taste)
   2 diced pickles
   1/3 cup mayonnaise
   1 small jar pimiento
   1 clove garlic (diced or rubbed on glass bowl)
   1/4 cup onions
   1/4 cup bell pepper (optional)


Boil potatoes and eggs about 45 minutes; dice.

Mix remaining ingredients.

Refrigerate.
