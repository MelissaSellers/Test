Jello

1983

Mix 1 large package jello with 4 cups hot water. Cool. Refrigerate several hours til jelled.

May serve with whipped cream, or may mix in fruit when almost jelled.

Hint: May use 2 cups ice for last two cups of hot water if you're in a hurry if jello is already dissolved.
