Easy Fruit Cobbler

1984

Cream together 1 cup sugar & 1 1/2 Tablespoons butter; add 3/4 cup milk with the following dry ingredients:

2 teaspoons baking powder
1 cup flour
1/8 teaspoon salt

Put batter in greased (large, shallow) baking dish; on top put 2 cups seeded berries / 1 can cherries / or 3 cups peaches.

Sprinkle on 1/2 cup sugar. Add 1/2 cup hot water over all.

Bake 30 minutes or more at 350°

Cool. May serve with cream and sugar. May top with vanilla ice cream.
