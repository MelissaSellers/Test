Apple Pie

1984

    6 apples, peeled and sliced
   3/4 cup sugar
   2 tablespoons flour
   1 teaspoon vanilla extract
   1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
   1 dash nutmeg
   1 dash salt
   2 tablespoons butter


Mix all ingredients & pour into unbaked pastry shell.

Bake at 350° for 45 minutes or til done.
