Tuna Sandwiches

   2 small cans tuna
   3 eggs, boiled and chopped
   2 medium pickles, chopped
   1 tablespoon onions, chopped (may omit)
   1/2 teaspoon salt
   1/2 teaspoon vinegar
   5 tablespoons mayonnaise


Mix above ingredients and spread on bread with mayonnaise. Serves 4.

May add lettuce and tomato to sandwich.
