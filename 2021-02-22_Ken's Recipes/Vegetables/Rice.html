Rice

1984

    1 cup rice, regular
   2 cups water
   1/2 teaspoon salt


Bring to boil (on double boiler, preferably, with 1/4" water in bottom pan); turn down to simmer.

Cook 15 - 20 minutes, stirring and adding a little water 1 or 2 times; add salt, butter, and serve.

With instant rice, bring water to boil; add rice; let set and steam with lid on and no heat for 5 minutes. Salt, butter, and serve.
