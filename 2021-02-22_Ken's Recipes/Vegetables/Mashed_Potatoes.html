Mashed Potatoes



Peel 4 large potatoes & cut in large chunks. Cover with water in pan. Salt (1 teaspoon). Boil on medium low heat, covered, about 20 minutes.

May need to add water. Fork will go thru easily when done.

Drain; mash; add 1/2 stick butter; add a 1/4 cup potato juice, 1/3 cup milk, and beat til light and fluffy.
