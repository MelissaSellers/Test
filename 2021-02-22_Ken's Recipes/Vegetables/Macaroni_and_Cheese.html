Macaroni and Cheese

1983

Directions are on pkg. or box.

Put macaroni in big pot of salted hot water. Boil 10 minutes on low.

Drain in collander. Add 1 Tablespoon butter, 1/2 cup milk, & 1/2 cup cheese; melt & serve.

Can add bacon, ham, peas, etc.
