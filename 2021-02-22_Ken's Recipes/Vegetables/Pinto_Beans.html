Pinto Beans

1983

Wash beans in collander; put in large pot. Add 2 chopped onions, 2 teaspoons salt, 1 pod garlic (chopped), and 1 cup salt pork or 4 - 6 strips of bacon.

Cook on low heat about 3 hours, keeping pot almost full of water.

Serve with cornbread, shallots, bacon, & salad.

* Add ingredients beyond beans & salt the next morning.
* 8 Cups of dried beans & 2 Tablespoons of salt. - Cook overnight on very low boil. (2) on our stove. *2017
