Baked Potato

1985

    4 large potatoes
   Worcestershire sauce
   Butter


Scrub potatoes; put butter and worcestershire sauce on potatoes. Wrap in foil.

Bake at 400° - 450° about 1 hour til potatoes feel soft when stuck with a fork.

Serve with butter, sour cream, cheese, bacon crisps.

Good with ham or barbequed chips.
