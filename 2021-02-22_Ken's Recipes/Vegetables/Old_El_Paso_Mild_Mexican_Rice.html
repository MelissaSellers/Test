Old El Paso Mild Mexican Rice

1985

    4 cups water
   2 tablespoons butter
   1 package Old El Paso Mexican Rice seasoning mix
   1 package Old El Paso Rice
   1/2 cup regular rice (not instant)


Mix all ingredients til stirred up. Boil water. Cover and simmer 20 minutes.

Remove from heat; stir & let stand uncovered 10 minutes til liquid is absorbed.
