Fried Eggs

1984

Heat 1/2 cup grease over medium heat.

Break 3 eggs into skillet. Put hot grease over eggs constantly with spatula.

When egg yolks look "white," take eggs out with spatula & drain grease off eggs with Scott towels.

Eat with toast, butter, jam; bacon or ham.
