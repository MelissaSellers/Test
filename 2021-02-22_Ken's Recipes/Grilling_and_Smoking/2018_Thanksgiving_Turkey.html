2018 Thanksgiving Turkey

   Make a brine:
   3 gallons water
   3 cups salt
   1 cup sugar


Remove "accessories" from turkey.

Soak turkey in brine overnight in refrigerator.

Soak wood chunks overnight.

The next morning, heat smoker to 250° F.

Keep smoker supplied with wood & water.

Turkey is done @ 170° F. inside. (Reached 180° in 2018)
